About Us

Who we are?

Olena Oleksiyivna Andriykiv was born in Chortkiv, Ternopil region, Ukraine in a family of military personnel. So, she studied and graduated with honors from the Medical College with a specialization in Obstetrics and Gynecology. From 2001-2016, she worked in clinics in Lviv and Kyiv.

Who we are?

During the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020-2021, Olena Andriykiv was part of a group of extreme medicine doctors who provided childbirth care to women infected with COVID-19. All mothers and babies she assisted survived.

Who we are?

Since February 2022, during the aggression of Russia against Ukraine, the clinic founded by Olena Andriykiv has been providing free medical services to female refugees, displaced persons, and female military personnel who were affected by the hostilities.

Who we are?

In 2022, Olena Andriykiv founded the Supporters of Ukraine Charitable Foundation, which carries out extensive charitable, educational, medical, and social programs. The motto of the Olena Andriykiv Charitable Foundation is "True patriotism, friendship, and love - sincere and quiet.”... - Read more

our mission

In this difficult time, our main mission is to join forces to support the vital activity of Ukrainian society. First of all, hospitals, schools and military hospitals. Ensuring peace and balance for families, communities, and society.  Another of our missions is to ensure development despite the war. Development of science, education, and art. It is important to prepare people in the post-war period. Formation of psychological adaptation of veterans, people with disabilities, avoidance and prevention of conflicts or domestic violence.

Our Vision

The vision of the organization is to build a system of assistance to people who have become victims of war. Coordinating the activities of medical professionals to create an international network of cooperation and exchange of experience in helping war victims. Exchange of practices of interaction between governmental and non-governmental organizations, self-government bodies to overcome the consequences of Russian aggression against Ukraine.  Creation of a permanent financial, organizational, cultural, educational and human resource to help rebuild Ukraine.

Our values

True patriotism, love and friendship - sincere and quiet. We win because we are together. Every day counts - every life matters. We don't ask for help, just share our ideas with those who need it.
